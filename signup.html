Honda Civic

Model:2023

  • Body style :

    Sedan

  • Engine:

    Standard: 158-horsepower 2.0-liter 4-cylinder engine

  • Technology:

    Infotainment: Standard 7-inch touchscreen with wired Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™. Upgraded Infotainment: Higher trims feature a 9-inch touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™. Audio Systems: Standard 8-speaker system; premium trims offer a Bose® premium sound system.

  • Safety:

    Standard Honda Sensing® safety technologies include: Forward Collision Warning (FCW) Collision Mitigation Braking System™ (CMBS™) Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) Lane Departure Warning (LDW) Lane Keeping Assist System (LKAS) Automatic High-Beam Headlights

  • Comfort:

    Available features include heated front seats, wireless charging, and a multi-angle rearview camera.

  • Performance & Efficiency:

    The standard engine provides 158 horsepower and 138 lb-ft of torque. The turbocharged engine produces 180 horsepower and 177 lb-ft of torque. Fuel economy is excellent, with ratings around 31 city/40 highway mpg on the LX trim.

Honda City

Model:2024

  • Body style :

    Sedan

  • Engine:

    1.5L i-VTEC petrol engine.

  • Exterior and Design

    Dimensions:
    The sedan measures approximately 4441 mm in length, 1694 mm in width, and 1498 mm in height.
    Wheelbase:
    The wheelbase is 2600 mm.
    Wheels:
    It features 15-inch steel rims with caps for the 1.2L variants, while higher trims like the RS hatchback may have 16-inch black alloy wheels.

  • Safety:

    Honda Sensing Suite:
    Higher-end models are equipped with the Honda Sensing Suite, which includes:
      Collision Mitigation Braking System (CMBS) Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) with Low-Speed Following Lane Keeping Assist System (LKAS) Road Departure Mitigation System (RDM) Auto High-Beam Headlights

  • Comfort:

    Available features include heated front seats, wireless charging, and a multi-angle rearview camera.

  • Performance & Efficiency:

    The standard engine provides 158 horsepower and 138 lb-ft of torque. The turbocharged engine produces 180 horsepower and 177 lb-ft of torque. Fuel economy is excellent, with ratings around 31 city/40 highway mpg on the LX trim.

Honda NSX

Model:2022

  • Body style :

    Sedan

  • Engine:

    A twin-turbocharged 3.5-liter V6 engine is paired with three electric motors, producing a combined 600 horsepower and 492 lb-ft of torque.

  • Performance:

    The hybrid system delivers rapid acceleration, enabling a 0-60 mph time of approximately 2.7 seconds.

  • Safety:

    standard driver-assistance features including front and rear parking sensors, a multi-angle rearview camera, Brake Assist, and Vehicle Stability Assist

  • Features and Design:

    Hybrid System:
    A combination of the V6 engine and three electric motors provides instant acceleration and G-force, with an electric motor adding torque to the V6 and another motor on each front wheel.
    Dynamic Modes:
    Includes 'Quiet', 'Sport', 'Sport+', and 'Track' modes, adjusting the driving dynamics for different environments, from quiet streets to a race track.
    Type S Enhancements:
    Chassis:
    Tighter chassis with adjustments to the torque vectoring system.
    Exterior:
    Redesigned front bumper, a larger carbon fiber ducktail spoiler, and a prominent rear diffuser.
    Wheels:
    New five-spoke alloy wheels.
    Tires:
    Super-sticky Pirelli P-Zero tires for enhanced grip.

  • Performance & Efficiency:

    The standard engine provides 158 horsepower and 138 lb-ft of torque. The turbocharged engine produces 180 horsepower and 177 lb-ft of torque. Fuel economy is excellent, with ratings around 31 city/40 highway mpg on the LX trim.
